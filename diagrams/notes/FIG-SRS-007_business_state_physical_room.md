# FIG-SRS-007 - Business State Diagram: Physical Room Operational Lifecycle

- Source block: DGM-STATE-ROOM-001.
- Skill used: `srs-drawio-business-state-diagram`.
- Output: `FIG-SRS-007_business_state_physical_room.drawio`.

This diagram shows the business lifecycle for a physical room from active availability through assignment, occupancy, cleaning, inspection, maintenance, out-of-service, and inactive states.

Covered states: Available, Assigned, Occupied, Dirty, Cleaning, Inspection Required, Maintenance, Out of Service, Inactive.

Covered transitions include room assignment, check-in, check-out, cleaning start/completion, inspection pass, issue reporting, maintenance resolution, out-of-service escalation, deactivation, and reactivation.
